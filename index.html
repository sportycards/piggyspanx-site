About PiggySpanx

Founded by creative visionary Kardyewest, PiggySpanx is an art studio and community. Our mission? To mash up the best of pop culture with wicked humor and high-quality craftsmanship. We produce short-run, handmade parody cards that don’t take themselves too seriously—but that collectors can’t get enough of.

Every card is meticulously designed, slabbed, and numbered. Our limited editions regularly sell out, so make sure to follow us on social media for drop announcements and behind‑the‑scenes peeks at upcoming series.
